The Business Model Canvas

Key Partners

Key Partners would be:

  • Neighbors (Customers)
  • Local Businesses (Revenue Providers)
  • Schools (content providers)
  • Police, Fire, Medical (content providers)

Key Activities

  • Community Members with Local Services (updates)
  • Community Members with Local Businesses (updates)
  • Revenue streams through Local Business advertising
  • Monthly Fees based on membership fees

Value Proposition

  • Safer Community
  • Convenience for members
  • Asset to local businesses (advertising)
  • Community Involvement

Customer Relationship

  • Neighbors with Neighbors
  • Businesses with Neighbors
  • local services with Neighbors

Customer Segments

  • Neighbors (monthly fees)
  • Businesses (advertising fees)

Key Resources

  • Users (Neighbors)
  • Advertising Fees
  • Membership Fees
  • User Content

Channels

  • Web
  • Mobile

Cost Structure

start small and grow based on response from users. In the future if the site is popular we can grow as necessary. The main goal of the site is a community service not so much a cash cow.

Revenue Streams

Advertising dollars from local businesses would be the main revenue stream. We could also charge a monthly fee for community members if the site provided enough befeficial content.
