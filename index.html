Key Activities

Bring a Service and a Tool to the local community which will help in many ways

Value Proposition

The value the site would bring among many things would be convenience, piece of mind, community involvement, pride in the neighborhood. The site could be a definite asset to businesses in the community through advertising on the site.

Customer Relationship

The site could bring the community together by having an area for posting local activities which would bring the community together.

Customer Segments

End User would be the community members living in the designated area the site designates. They would elect a board to determine what goes on the site.

Key Resources

Community involvement the site will only be as successful as the community members make it

Channels

web & mobile

Cost Structure

start small and grow based on user usage. In the future if the site is popular we can use advertising dollars to help maintain site

Revenue Streams

Advertising dollars from local businesses would be the main revenue stream. We could also branch the site out to many different communities or areas.
